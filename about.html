About Us

We're a small, passionate team of tech enthusiasts serving the Greenwich community since 2018.

Greenwich's go-to tech accessories store

Founded in 2018, Greenwich Gadget Hub started as a small kiosk in Greenwich Market. Today, we operate from our dedicated store on Greenwich High Road, stocking over 200 tech products from trusted brands.

Our mission is simple: to make premium tech accessories accessible, affordable, and well-supported. Whether you're a student, remote worker, or tech enthusiast, we have what you need.

We pride ourselves on expert advice, fast click-and-collect service, and a friendly, no-pressure shopping experience.
